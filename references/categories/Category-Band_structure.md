# Category:Band structure


## Theory\[<a
href="/wiki/index.php?title=Category:Band_structure&amp;veaction=edit&amp;section=1"
class="mw-editsection-visualeditor"
title="Edit section: Theory">edit</a> \| (./index.php.md)\]

The band structure describes the eigenvalues of the orbitals throughout
the Brillouin zone. It gives a detailed insight into the material
determining many optical and transport properties. A band structure is
often visualized along high symmetry paths. Some external
tools[^bilbao:kvec-1][^seekpath-2]
help to identify these points for materials of any symmetry.
