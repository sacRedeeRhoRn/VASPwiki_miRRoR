This tutorial explains how to calculate the X-ray absorption spectrum
(XAS) of a given compound in particular the edge and near edge region.
The respective spectroscopy is often called XANES (X-ray absorption near
edge structure) or NEXAFS (near edge X-ray absorption fine structure).

This tutorial requires VASP 6.0 or higher.
