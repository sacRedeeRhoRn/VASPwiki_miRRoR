# LSCSGRAD


LSCSGRAD = .TRUE. \| .FALSE.  
Default: **LSCSGRAD** = .TRUE. 

Description: LSCSGRAD  decides
whether to compute gradients in the calculation of the MBD dispersion
energy or the SCS contribution.
