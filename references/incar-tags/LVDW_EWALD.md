# LVDW_EWALD
LVDW_EWALD = logical  
Default: **LVDW_EWALD** = .FALSE. 

Description: LVDW_EWALD decides whether the lattice summation in
$E_{disp}$ expression by means of
Ewald's summation is computed.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

LVDW_EWALD decides whether the lattice summation in
$E_{disp}$ expression by means of
Ewald's summation is computed in the pairwise van der Waals corrections
schemes [DFT-D2](../methods/DFT-D2.md),
[DFT-ulg](../methods/DFT-ulg.md) and [Tkatchenko-Scheffler
method](../methods/Tkatchenko-Scheffler_method.md).
