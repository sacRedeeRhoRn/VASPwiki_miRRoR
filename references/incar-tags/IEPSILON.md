# IEPSILON
IEPSILON = 1 \| 2 \| 3 \| 4 

Description: IEPSILON determines along which Cartesian direction the E
field is applied.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

- IEPSILON=1 x direction
- IEPSILON=2 y direction
- IEPSILON=3 z direction
- IEPSILON=4 x, y and z direction; three indpendent calculations are
  performed

|                                                                         |
|-------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Important:** IEPSILON only applies to the time-propagation algorithm. |
