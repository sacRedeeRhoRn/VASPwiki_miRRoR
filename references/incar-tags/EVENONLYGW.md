# EVENONLYGW
EVENONLYGW = \[logical\]  
Default: **EVENONLYGW** = .FALSE. 

Description: EVENONLYGW allows to restrict the k-points in the
evaluation of response functions (in [GW
calculations](../redirects/GW_calculations.md)) to even values.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

EVENONLYGW=*.TRUE.* is only of limited use and restricts the summation
to k-points with $n_{1}+n_{2}+n_{3}$
being even ($\Gamma$ point and from
there on every second k-point included).
