# EVENONLYGW


EVENONLYGW = \[logical\]  
Default: **EVENONLYGW** = .FALSE. 

Description: EVENONLYGW allows
to restrict the k-points in the evaluation of response functions (in
<a href="/wiki/GW_calculations" class="mw-redirect"
title="GW calculations">GW calculations</a>) to even values.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

EVENONLYGW=*.TRUE.* is only of
limited use and restricts the summation to k-points with
$n_{1}+n_{2}+n_{3}$ being even
($\Gamma$ point and from there on every second k-point
included).
