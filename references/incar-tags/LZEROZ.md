# LZEROZ


LZEROZ = .TRUE. \| .FALSE.  
Default: **LZEROZ** = .FALSE. 

Description: for LZEROZ=.TRUE.
the *z*-component of the spin-spiral magnetisation density will be
forced to be and to remain zero.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

See the [description of spin spiral
calculations](../theory/Spin_spirals.md) for more details.
