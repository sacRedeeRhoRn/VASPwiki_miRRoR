# LSCALER0
LSCALER0 = .TRUE. \| .FALSE.  
Default: **LSCALER0** = .TRUE. 

Description: LSCALER0  decides whether to re-scale the van der Waals
radii $R_{0}$ (LSCALER0=*.TRUE.*) or
not.
