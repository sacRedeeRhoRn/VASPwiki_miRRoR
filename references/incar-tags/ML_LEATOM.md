# ML_LEATOM
ML_LEATOM = \[logical\]  
Default: **ML_LEATOM** = .FALSE. 

Description: This term specifies whether the total atomic energy is
written out or not.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

This tag specifies whether the total atomic energy (potential energy
plus kinetic energy) for each MD step is written out to the
[ML_EATOM](../output-files/ML_EATOM.md) file. It also writes the atomic
positions and lattice parameters at every MD step to this file.
