# FINDIFF
FINDIFF = \[integer\]  
Default: **FINDIFF** = 1 

Description: Defines whether a forward (FINDIFF=1) or a central
(FINDIFF=2) difference formula for the numerical differentiation to
compute the curvature along the dimer direction is used in the [improved
dimer method](../misc/Improved_Dimer_Method.md).
