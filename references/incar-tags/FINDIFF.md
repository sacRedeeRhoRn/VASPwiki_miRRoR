# FINDIFF


FINDIFF = \[integer\]  
Default: **FINDIFF** = 1 

Description: Defines whether a forward
(FINDIFF=1) or a central
(FINDIFF=2) difference formula
for the numerical differentiation to compute the curvature along the
dimer direction is used in the
<a href="/wiki/Improved_Dimer_Method" class="mw-redirect"
title="Improved Dimer Method">improved dimer method</a>.
