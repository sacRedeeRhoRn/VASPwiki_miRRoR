# Adsorption of H2O on TiO2


## Task\[<a
href="/wiki/index.php?title=Adsorption_of_H2O_on_TiO2&amp;veaction=edit&amp;section=1"
class="mw-editsection-visualeditor"
title="Edit section: Task">edit</a> \| (./index.php.md)\]

In this example you will investigate the adsorption of H<sub>2</sub>O on
a TiO<sub>2</sub> surface by means of standard geometry optimisation
("relaxation") and by means of constrained molecular dynamics.
